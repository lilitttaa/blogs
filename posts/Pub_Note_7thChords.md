## What is 7th Chords

7th Chords 简单来说就是 Triads（三和弦）在对应 Scale 上加上一个 7th，主要分为一下几种：

- Major 7
- Dominant 7
- Minor 7
- Minor 7th Flat 5/ Half Diminished 7
- Diminished 7

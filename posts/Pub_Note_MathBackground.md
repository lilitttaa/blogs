## Vector

- vector : $\vec{a} = \begin{bmatrix} a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} a_1 \\ a_2 \\ a_3 \end{bmatrix}^T$
- magnitude/length/norm : $||\vec{a}|| = \sqrt{a_1^2 + a_2^2 + a_3^2}$
- unit vector : $\hat{a} = \frac{\vec{a}}{||\vec{a}||}$
